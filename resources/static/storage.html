Inspect Local Storage

The editor uses the local storage of your browser to keep a copy of records you save before sending it to the server. This safeguards your work if the server can't be reached or has problems. However the browser puts limits on this storage. This page helps:

  1. to clean out the local storage of records in your browser by using clean all or delete specific entries;
  2. by limiting the number of backups per editor
You can also download backup entries (for the latest one per editor the link is in bold). Share it with your editor administrator to salvage your work.

Contact your editor administrator or the DI/Structured Data team (structured-data@di.huc.knaw.nl)!
